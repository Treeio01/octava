FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Is this just a trading bot?
No. Octava is an autonomous AI investment agent focused on full strategy management, not just trades.
Do I control the assets?
You control the wallet. Octava manages the plan — based on the goal you define.
Where is the money held?
In a non-custodial wallet generated for your strategy. Only you receive the private key.
Is it secure?
Yes. Octava never touches funds directly. Only you have access to your wallet keys.
What’s the minimum amount to start?
$50 equivalent in SOL is the minimum. No upper limit.
Can I exit anytime?
Yes. Just type “stop” in chat — Octava will wind down the strategy and return the balance.
Is this DeFi?
Partly. Octava uses DeFi under the hood, but you never have to understand it — the AI handles it for you.
Can I run more than one strategy?
Yes. You can run up to 3 active strategies at once — each in its own dedicated chat.
